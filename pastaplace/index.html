Services

Open 7 days a week from 5pm onwards.

We are a family owned and operated restaurant who have been making home-made pasta and sauces since 1988.
Our services include:

  • home delivery
  • gluten free and vegetarian options
  • BYO alcohol
  • indoor and outdoor dining
  • a wide selection of cakes and gelato

Banquets are available for bookings of 10 or more people. Lunch time functions can also be arranged.
